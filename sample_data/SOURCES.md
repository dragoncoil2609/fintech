# Sample data — sources & licensing

All files in `sample_data/` are **synthetic** and released under **CC0** (public
domain). No real personal or financial data is included.

| File | What it is | How it was made |
|---|---|---|
| `sample_statement.csv` | 58 transactions across Apr–Jun 2026 (income, bills, food, transport, shopping, entertainment, health, education, transfers) with realistic Vietnamese merchants (Highlands, GRAB, Shopee, EVN, FPT, BigC, KFC, Galaxy Cinema…). | Hand-authored synthetic data. |
| `sample_receipts/*.pdf` | 3 Vietnamese retail receipts (Lotte Mart, Highlands, BigC). | Generated by `scripts/gen_receipts.py` (reportlab). |
| `smoke_test_5_rows.csv`, `bank_statement_q2_2026.csv` | Smaller smoke fixtures. | Synthetic. |

Merchant names are used nominally to make categorization realistic; no affiliation
or endorsement is implied.
